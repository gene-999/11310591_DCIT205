Welcome

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at University of Ghana. UG has been at the forefront of education in Ghana ever since its inception. Our department is home to over 30 faculty members, with strong groups in theory, networks & systems, database, architecture, compilers, programming languages, security, machine learning and many more.
