This website provides an overview of the undergraduate programs offered by the Department of Computer Science, University of Ghana.

Undergraduate Programmes

The Department of Computer Science, University of Ghana offers Bachelor of Science (BSc.) in Computer Science and Bachelor of Science (BSc.) in Information Technolog. The BSc. is a 4-year (full-time) programme that covers both the theoretical and practical aspects of computer science. Students enrolled in these programme learn how to use computer science as a tool to solve real-world problems in various domains. They also acquire skills in advanced topics such as databases, data structures and algorithms, and networks. By the end of this programme, students will be able to design, implement, and evaluate computer systems and applications that are efficient, reliable, and secure.
