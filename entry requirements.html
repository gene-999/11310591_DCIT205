Entry Requirements

Bachelor of Science in Mathematical Sciences

Mathematical Sciences (Actuarial Science, Biomathematics, Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Physics) Core: Credit passes in English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science & Social Studies Electives: Credit passes in Elective Mathematics and any other two subjects Bachelor of Science in Information Technology Core: Credit passes in English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science & Social Studies Electives: Credit passes in any three subjects.

MSc Computer science

The minimum entry is a First Degree in Computing, IT and any relevant field from a recognised University.

MPhil Computer science

The minimum entry requirement is a good first degree (at least a second class lower division) in Computer Science or a related field from a recognised University. the MPhil is a 2-year (full-time) programme. The first year of the MPhil is taught course and second year is research and dissertation.

Ph.D.

Applicants with very good first degree from the University of Ghana or any recognized/accredited University may apply for a PhD programme.
